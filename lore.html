Lore of Esel

The history of Esel is filled with myths, ancient wars, lost kingdoms, and powerful magic that still affects the world today.

The First Age

Long before the current kingdoms, Esel was ruled by magical beings who shaped the land with powerful spells.

The Shattering

A great magical disaster broke apart old alliances and created many of the dangerous ruins found across Esel.

The Age of Crowns

After the Shattering, humans, elves, dwarves, and other peoples built new kingdoms from the remains of the old world.
