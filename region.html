Regions of Esel

Esel is made up of many different lands, each with its own dangers, cultures, and mysteries.

The Elderwood

A massive ancient forest filled with old magic, hidden spirits, and ruins from a forgotten age.

The Crownlands

The political center of Esel, home to castles, noble families, and constant struggles for power.

The Ash Wastes

A dangerous ruined land scarred by magical disaster and filled with monsters, treasure, and secrets.
